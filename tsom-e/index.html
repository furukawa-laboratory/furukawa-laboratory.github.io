  • Maps can be colored by two ways.
    • U-Matrix: Cluster borders are indicated by red regions.
    • Component Plane: The high/low score regions are indicated by red/blue respectively.
  • By clicking the mouse button on a map, you can specify the conditioning point for each map.
    • Conditioning points are indicated by circles.
    • When one or more maps are conditioned, the other maps are colored with respect to the conditioning point. For example, if a point in the user map is selected, then the beverage map and the situation map are colored according to the scores of the selected users.
    • Click the conditioning point again, then it becomes unselected.
    • By dragging mouse cursor with pressing the left button, you can move the conditioning point continuously.
